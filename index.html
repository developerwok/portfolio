Projects

Wrangle.me

Creative teams use emails to discuss projects and share assets. Staying organized across multple channels and platforms is challenging and often can be a barrier to accomplishing goals.

From extensive user interviews, the Wrangle team decided to build a tool to manage conversations around visual assets and discussion topics.

With Wrangle you can quickly import assets from Dropbox or easily add search results from the web. The ability to tag, categorize and export to pdf makes collecting, curating and presenting ideas a fluid process for creative teams.

Help Kelp

The Kelp Challenge is an enviromental restoration project. The project aims to mitigate the degradation of kelp forest located in the Santa Monica Bay, with the ultimate goal of acheiving full kelp forest restoration.

Spearheaded by the Climate Cents team, under the guidance of the Bay Foundation, the project needed someone to realize the wireframes and implement the designer's complex ui interactions. The challenge of making a parralax based site with a mulititude of animation elements was rewarding.

My pro-bono contribution helped Climate Cents get accepted into the University of Southern California Code for Change program.

Code the Change is a student organization at USC that drives social impact through software projects for non profit organizations.

Newbie Gardening E-book

Gardening is often an intimidating experiences for those that have not invested the time or have the space to learn how to grow plants. The plant world is diverse.

I wanted to summarize the information I was learning into a useful digital pamplet for those at the begining of there learning journey.

Whisper node

Whisper Node is a lighting panel that reacts to audio input.

I was responsible for the programming of a prototype designed for an interactive lighting system. Using audio input as the driver for interaction, I created an algortihm to map the current volume of sound to intensity of light. This was layered on top a secondary layer of light that was mapped to historical recording of sound.
